## 🧠 About the Project

**MockMentor AI** is not just another mock interview tool — it's a **personal AI interviewer** designed to simulate real-world interview scenarios.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional, this platform adapts to your profile and helps you **identify gaps, improve answers, and build confidence**.

---

## ✨ What Makes It Different?

🔹 AI-generated interview questions based on your role & experience  
🔹 Dynamic difficulty adjustment (not static questions)  
🔹 Real-time structured answers using AI  
🔹 Performance insights + improvement suggestions  
🔹 Clean dashboard to track your progress  

---

## 🎯 Core Features

- 🤖 **AI Interview Engine**  
  Powered by Gemini AI to generate intelligent Q&A sessions

- 🎯 **Role-Based Interviews**  
  Customize interviews (Frontend, Backend, HR, etc.)

- 📊 **Smart Feedback System**  
  Get actionable insights instead of generic responses

- 🧩 **Experience-Based Questions**  
  Questions adapt based on your years of experience

- 🗂️ **Interview History Tracking**  
  Revisit and analyze past interviews anytime

- ⚡ **Fast & Responsive UI**  
  Built for smooth and modern user experience
